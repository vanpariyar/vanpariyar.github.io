In a world filled with fast-paced thrillers and modern dramas, every now and then, a show comes along that completely grounds you and connects you to something deeper. If you are looking for a meaningful, soulful, and spiritually uplifting watch, I highly recommend checking out “Shri Bhaktamal Gatha” on YouTube.

The first episode, “Shri Nabhadass Ji Charitra (Part 1),” is hosted on the channel Swami Karun Dass Ji Maharaj, and it sets the stage for a beautiful television series based on a 500-year-old sacred text.

You can watch the first episode directlyhere.

What is “Bhaktamal Gatha” About?

The series is based on Bhaktamal, a historic poetic text composed roughly 500 years ago by the revered saint Shri Nabhadass Ji Maharaj [02:27]. This monumental work chronicles the inspiring life stories, devotion, and struggles of hundreds of saints and devotees across different eras.

What makes this series truly beautiful is that it isn’t just about Kings and popular figures. As Swami Karun Dass Ji mentions in the introduction, it showcases saints from all walks of life—sanyasis, householders (grihasth), the wealthy, and the impoverished across all backgrounds [03:15].

The Masterful Research Behind the Series

One of the reasons this series stands out is the incredible depth of research behind it. The creators didn’t just stop at the original text. They integrated deep historical references published by Gita Press Gorakhpur (compiled by Bhai Ji Shri Hanuman Prasad Poddar Ji) [04:14].

Even more fascinating, the production team traveled to ancient monasteries (samadhis) to gather unprinted, hidden historical accounts preserved directly by the lineage disciples of these saints [04:50]. This means when you watch this show, you are seeing rare, authentic spiritual history brought to life for the very first time on screen!

The Gripping Opening Story: Shri Nabhadass Ji

The first episode introduces us to the author of the text himself, Nabhadass Ji. Born without eyesight, his story begins with a profound conversation on the nature of existence: “If you are the body made of five elements, so am I. If you are the soul connected to the Supreme, so am I” [06:49].

Through heartwarming and tearful moments, we see his parents grappling with his visual impairment, only for his father to realize that Nabhadass’s purpose is divine. His father beautifully proclaims that though his son cannot see the world, he will be the one to show the path of truth to generations to come [16:15]. The episode further dives into emotional family struggles, testing times of drought, and unwavering faith in the Divine [22:05].

Why I Recommend Watching It:

  • Deeply Emotional & Inspiring: The storytelling captures human vulnerability, familial love, and absolute devotion flawlessly.
  • Authentic and Pure: Unlike highly commercialized dramas, this series retains its cultural purity and profound philosophical depth.
  • Soul-Stirring Music: The introductory Bhajan and title track perfectly set a meditative and peaceful tone for the viewer [00:25].

Start Watching Today!

Whether you are a spiritual seeker, a lover of history, or simply looking for wholesome content to watch with your family, Shri Bhaktamal Gatha is a masterpiece worth your time.
